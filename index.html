Welcome to Telu FC

The first teenage football club in Osun State

At a Glance

Telu FC was established in March 2020 to discover young soccer talents and make them better persons in the society. It was created to challenge the traditional concepts and approaches of young people's experience in football.
We believe that football coaching is not only about producing the best players but to help young footballers achieve their aims. With our networks of International football clubs and football management organizations, we planned to help young players with opportunities to showcase their talents to football coaches and agents around the world.

At Telu FC

The club yearns to ripen intelligent, self-assured young people in a professional and exciting atmosphere, giving them the skills to rise and seek greater ambitions in their lives with success.

No doubt, learning without fun is compromised and a young person's desire and pleasure for the game can weaken. At the academy, we are always determined to be better and make sure that our players in every age group are having fun and enjoying their time and stay at the academy.
